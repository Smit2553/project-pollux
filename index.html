About The Project

The Pollux E-bike is a reliable and versatile mode of transportation that combines the convenience of a traditional bicycle with the added power of an electric motor. This ebike is perfect for commuting to work or running errands, as it allows you to effortlessly navigate through traffic and tackle steep hills with ease. With its sleek design and durable construction, the Pollux ebike is a reliable choice for anyone looking to incorporate more sustainable transportation into their daily routine.

Computer Aided Design

10/12 - 11/19

● We began with the team creating a basic outline and sketch of the e-bike. This initial sketch was then used to create a more detailed 3D model using a specialized CAD software called Solidworks. We specifed the dimensions, materials, and other properties of the object, and manipulated the model in order to perfect its design.

Circuit Design

10/12 - 11/19

● We spent some time thinking about how we wanted to convert power from the dynamo into 5v power to charge regular electronic devices. We decided to use a custom designed 12v-5v voltage converter. We used a piece of software called LT Spice to simulate the circuit and make sure it would work as intended.

Refinement

11/19 - Present Day

● Of course as with any project the design of the project was not perfect the first time around. We spent a few weeks trying and fine tuning and polishing the design. As of now, we are still working on the project. We are currently working on the final design of the e-bike.
